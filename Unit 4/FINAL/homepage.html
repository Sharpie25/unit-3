Zac Brown Band is a pop-country band that originated in Georgia. They've been around and touring since 2004 and only grew from there. In 2008, they released one of their top albums, The Foundation. After that they've released other award winning albums, won Grammys, and toured with other big arists like Kenney Chesney.
