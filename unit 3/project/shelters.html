Adopt From Shelters

Many animals- specifically cats and dogs- are neglected at pet shops. This is mainly because when some people go to adopt an animal, they have a specifc animal in mind. These people end up going to breeders instead of searching for animals who already need homes.

To read more about adopting verses shopping, click on the "More Information" link below.

If you are interested in adopting an animal, click the "Shelters in the Area" link to find animals shelters in the Detriot area.
